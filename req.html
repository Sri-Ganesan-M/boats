How to Make a Paper Boat

  1. Take a rectangular piece of paper.
  2. Fold it in half lengthwise.
  3. Fold it again widthwise and crease it.
  4. Shape the folds into a triangle by tucking in the edges.
  5. Fold the bottom edges up to form the boat.
  6. Open the folds carefully to reveal your paper boat!
